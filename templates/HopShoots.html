Hop Shoots (scientific name : humulus-lupulus )

Hop shoots are cone-shaped, green-coloured flowers of female hop plants.Hop shoots fruits, flowers, and stems are useful in making:
  • Beverages
  • Beer
  • Antibiotics
  • Medicines
  • Aromatic products

Hop plants germinate from the soil during the month of March.

Hops cultivation in India, there should be: Minimum of 120 days without frost.

Loamy, well-drained soil, is best for hops cultivation. Soil with a ph level between 6 to 7.0 is best suited for crop growth.

Adding legume cover crops and compost can help provide the required nutrients to the crop. Hops require nitrogen up to 200 lbs (224kg/ha) in about a 6-week short window.

Initial investment is of around 3 lakhs.

Yield 2-5 t/ha
